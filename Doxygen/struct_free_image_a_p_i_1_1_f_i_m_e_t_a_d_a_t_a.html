Detailed Description

The FIMETADATA structure is an unique search handle for metadata search operations.

The FIMETADATA structure is usually returned by the FreeImageAPI.FreeImage.FindFirstMetadata(FREE_IMAGE_MDMODEL, FIBITMAP, out FITAG) function and then used on subsequent calls to FreeImageAPI.FreeImage.FindNextMetadata(FIMETADATA, out FITAG). When the FIMETADATA handle is no longer used, it needs to be freed by the FreeImageAPI.FreeImage.FindCloseMetadata(FIMETADATA) function.
